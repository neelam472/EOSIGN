Video Demonstration of EOSIGN:

In this supplementary material, we provide two videos to demonstrate the performance of our proposed system for sign language generation. These videos highlight the differences between traditional multi-signer recordings, which often exhibit temporal inconsistencies and a synthetically generated single-signer video created using our framework.

Figure 1: Multiple Signers Video

Multiple Signers Video contains real-world recordings of multiple signers performing different signs. This video showcases the natural variation in signing styles, hand movements and facial expressions. However, due to the use of multiple signers and varying recording conditions, the video exhibits temporal inconsistencies such as abrupt transitions, timing variations, and occasional disruptions in gesture fluidity. These inconsistencies arise from differences in individual signing speeds and styles, leading to unnatural transitions between signers. This video serves as a benchmark for comparing the fluidity and temporal consistency of natural sign language to the output of our synthetic model.

Single Signer Video demonstrates the output of our proposed system, where a single signer performs various signs. In contrast to the traditional multi-signer video, our proposed system ensure temporal consistency throughout the single-signer process.
